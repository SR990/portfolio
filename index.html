PROJECTS



MEDBOT

Medbot is an intelligent virtual assistant designed to help users manage their health. It begins by analyzing symptoms to assess the user's condition and provides basic prescriptions for common ailments. Medbot also sets reminders for taking medications and uses age and gender detection to offer personalized health advice. In addition, it suggests nearby hospitals and provides routes to medical facilities for easy access. Alongside its healthcare features, Medbot can perform everyday tasks like launching apps, playing videos, and opening websites, making it a versatile tool for both health management and general use.

Color Recognition

I developed an application that identifies and displays the colors detected as you scroll the pointer over different areas of the screen. The app provides real-time feedback, showing the RGB (Red, Green, Blue) and Hexadecimal values of the colors, along with their exact names. This tool is especially useful for designers and developers needing precise color identification for digital work. With its intuitive interface, the application enhances efficiency by simplifying color selection and recognition.
